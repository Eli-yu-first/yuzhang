  $Innovation = \left( \frac{\sqrt{(Knowledge \cdot Experience)^2 + (Collaboration \cdot Creativity)^2}}{Time + Resources} \right)^{Adaptability} \\+ \int_{0}^{Goals} \frac{Vision \cdot Execution}{Obstacles + \delta} \, d\text{effort}$
</div>
